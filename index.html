ABOUT US

The Love letter was started by myself five years ago when I discovered how fun making quote prints was over the Christmas break. I got quite good, making them for family and friends then eventually clients via Instagram. 7 months in I recklessly decided to leave my job because we suffer for the art you know? Thus, The Love Letter was born.

Five years on we are a team of 8 working tirelessly to bring you new and fun pre made prints, as well as custom print made to suit you.
